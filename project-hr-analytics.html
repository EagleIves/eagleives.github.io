HR Analytics Dashboard

A comprehensive Power BI solution for workforce management, providing actionable insights across headcount, turnover, leave, attendance, and performance metrics

2024-2025
Business Project
Power BI, DAX

Project Overview

OBJECTIVE

The objective was to create a comprehensive HR analysis dashboard in Power BI where HR officers can gain quick insights into a wide array of staff statistics, including headcount, leave utilisation, employee turnover, time and attendance, and more. Imagine a one-stop shop that answers all your staff-related questions. The dashboard aims to empower decision-makers with actionable information for effective workforce management, retention, succession planning, and overall employee satisfaction.

Dashboard Pages

Overview Page

Acts as a brief preview for upper management consumption, providing high-level understanding of the workforce. Touches on headcount, staff movement, demographics, leave statistics, location and makeup of staff. Perfect for executive briefings and strategic planning sessions.

HR Analytics Dashboard - Overview Page showing key workforce metrics

Headcount & Diversity

Delves deeper into workforce characteristics, illuminating gender makeup, unionisation percentage, average tenure, and period to retirement. Statistics can be drilled down by Division, Department, Functional Unit, Cadre, Job Category, Location and Date of Joining.

Key Insight: Majority of staff have tenure of less than 5 years, prompting discussions on turnover rate and measures to improve employee satisfaction.

Headcount & Diversity analysis with demographic breakdowns

Annual Leave Summary

Features KPIs for leave liability, leave utilisation rate and end-of-year leave balance target. KPIs calculated on a prorated basis, allowing leave statistics to be viewed up to the period under review.

Key Insights:

  • • Leave utilisation rate steadily increases as the year progresses
  • • Select few employees inflate average leave liability by utilizing little to no leave
  • • By September 2025, utilisation at 47% vs 75% target - urgent action needed

Annual Leave Summary with prorated KPIs and utilization tracking

Staff Movement Summary

Turnover analysis featuring KPIs such as new entrants, exits, internal hires, average tenure of active and inactive employees. Includes distribution of exit reasons and designation analysis.

Key Insight: Exits doubling the number of entrants may point to company strategy to reduce labour costs.

Staff Movement Summary tracking turnover, exits, and new entrants

Time & Attendance

Tracks attendance habits of full-time office staff with KPIs for average time-in, time-out, work hours, attendance rate and standard workdays. Integrated with leave dataset for accuracy. Attendance rates displayed by department with drill-down to individual employees.

Time & Attendance tracking with detailed metrics by department

Goal Setting Status

Tracks progress of performance appraisal process throughout the company as conducted within the HRM system. Can be utilised by department heads with row-level security to monitor their teams.

Key Insights:

  • • 478 of 556 staff enrolled for appraisal (78 exempted)
  • • 91% staff submitted self-evaluations
  • • 90% supervisors completed subordinate evaluations
  • • 87% reviewers submitted evaluations
  • • Overall 91% completion rate

Goal Setting Status tracking appraisal completion rates

End-of-Year Goal Review Evaluation

Analyzes how well employees have achieved year-start goals. Average ratings between 2-4, with most at midpoint of 3.

Key Insight: Motors division skewed towards lower ratings (2-3), while Hospitality division showed higher ratings (3-4).

Business Impact

  • Enhanced Decision-Making: Provides HR officers and leadership with instant access to critical workforce metrics, enabling data-driven strategic decisions.
  • Improved Workforce Planning: Turnover analysis and tenure tracking enable proactive succession planning and retention strategies.
  • Leave Liability Management: Prorated calculations and utilization tracking help minimize year-end leave liabilities and ensure compliance.
  • Performance Visibility: Real-time tracking of appraisal completion rates ensures accountability and timely performance reviews.
  • Attendance Accountability: Integration with leave data and shift-specific analysis enables accurate attendance monitoring and compliance tracking.
